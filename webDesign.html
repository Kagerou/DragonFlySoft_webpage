We keep your content consistent and responsive

At DragonFlySoft we know how important it is to deliver the same content and user experience to everyone who visits your site. That's why all our websites are built to elegantly resize themselves, so no matter how you view your site nothing gets left out.

We also realize not every browser supports every flashy feature, and take care to build a reliable base for your site that works anywhere. Special features are built on top of that base so no one is left looking at a broken web page.

SEO

In the sea of web pages it can be difficult for your page to be found. That's why we take extra care to optimize your site for search engines.

  • We help you choose the right keywords to make your site pop.
  • We use semantic coding.
  • And we don't split your search ratings between PC and mobile sites.

HTML5

The future of the web is all about HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. All the major browsers support it. It's on mobile devices, PC's, and soon even on your TV.

We build all our sites using HTML5 and do our best to follow the WC3's standards. Ultimately, this will mean new features will be able to be added to your site easily when they become available.

Social Media

When was the last time you went a day without looking at a social media site? How about your customers? Social media is an ever more important method for getting your message out to the world. We'll help you set up your social media and integrate them with your website.

Analytics

In order to get the most out of your website you need to know which parts are attracting attention and which parts are ignored. We will set up and embed Google Analytics into your site so you see how much traffic you're getting and where it's coming from. Or if you like, we can watch it for you.
