About Us

DragonFlySoft is a small design company started in 2012 with the aim of helping businesses make the transition to the mobile world. We understand the need to have content available on a wide variety of devices, and we aim to meet that need.

Our head office is in Japan, but we have embraced the mobile world and do much of our work while mobile. What this means for you is that we are readily available to talk with you through video chat (Skype, Line) or boring old email. You can drop by our Google+, Facebook, and Twitter pages as well to say hi.
